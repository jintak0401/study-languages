## 🔑 Key rules learned today
1. **talk with / to people** — never "talk people".
2. **people from other countries** — not "other country's people".
3. **want + someone + to + verb** — "I want you **to** build it".
4. **make a mistake** — never "do a mistake".
5. **every + singular noun** — "every mistake".
6. **spend time** — not "invest time".
7. **a / an / the** — singular countable nouns need an article.
8. **whether (~인지 아닌지)** — "I don't know **whether** it will work."

## 💬 Phrase of the day

Different ways to say it:
